the plush

Mother's day may have passed, but this mother is serving no matter the day! Otsuka Ray has left the screen and jumped into the 3D world for her Youtooz plush debut! Sitting at 9 inches tall with her legs kicked forward and holding her daughter Reika in her lap, she's dressed in a black skirt and shoes along with a frilly white shirt. Wearing a bow around her neck that matches the one on her back, lavender hair falls down either side of her blushing face as Reika wears a yellow plaid skirt. This plush is packed to the seams with 100% PP cotton and is made of minky and tricot materials with accents of felt, perfect for cuddling or raising kids with!

About otsuka Ray

Otsuka Ray is a Japanese VTuber and singer known for her warm presence and expressive voice. Since debuting in 2019, she's built a welcoming community through karaoke streams, gaming, and heartfelt conversations that blend comfort with authenticity.
